# Warm Institutional Serif

Source signal: Reference B.

## Use When

Use for healthcare, education, civic systems, high-trust AI, legal, research, or
institutional products that need warmth without softness.

## Surface

- ivory or warm off-white paper
- rust, clay, or oxblood accent
- large flood sections can carry proof
- blurred warm media can support credibility
- hairline separators and calm grids

## Typography

- display: editorial serif
- body: readable serif or quiet sans
- labels: uppercase micro labels
- stats: large serif numerals

## Dominant Gesture

One of:

- large serif trust statement
- rust statistic field
- institutional proof wall
- warm media object

## Interaction Stance

- quiet
- slow reveal
- restrained hover
- no kinetic gimmicks

## Good Components

- editorial logo wall
- numbered stat strip
- pull quote
- warm case-study panel
- minimal CTA
- serif masthead

## Avoid

- sterile futuristic healthcare UI
- overbright blue trust palette
- fake hospital logos
- invented capacity metrics
- childish wellness illustrations
