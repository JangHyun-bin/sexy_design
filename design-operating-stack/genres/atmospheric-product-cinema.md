# Atmospheric Product Cinema

Source signal: Reference C.

## Use When

Use for community, creator, education, collaboration, and consumer SaaS products
that should feel like a place or mood before they feel like software.

## Surface

- dark scenic or cinematic backdrop
- cyan, lavender, green, or purple light
- soft grain
- black continuation sections
- product screen embedded as a scene object

## Typography

- display: soft geometric sans
- body: neutral sans with low contrast
- labels: small pill labels or subtle captions

## Dominant Gesture

One of:

- product floating in a landscape
- luminous phone or app surface
- atmospheric hero scene
- dark-to-light cinematic fold

## Interaction Stance

- slow cinematic reveal
- parallax only if it supports depth
- soft transitions
- no abrupt decorative animation

## Good Components

- cinematic product fold
- floating product mockup
- soft CTA pill
- dark editorial text block
- community member surface
- large media band

## Avoid

- overexplaining above the fold
- generic dashboard cards
- random aurora gradients
- too many saturated colors
- marketing copy that breaks the mood
