# Mobile Kinetic Finance

Source signal: alarm, wallet, banking, and crypto mobile screens.

## Use When

Use for mobile apps where time, money, state, cards, transactions, or tactile
controls are the product experience.

## Surface

- phone-native gradients or solid flood colors
- black-to-blue finance depth
- acid yellow, red, lavender, or blue as a controlled accent
- frosted panels
- dot matrix or ruler-like controls
- large white sheets over dark upper fields

## Typography

- display: oversized numeric or heavy sans
- body: compact mobile sans
- labels: small functional captions
- amounts and times must dominate when relevant

## Dominant Gesture

One of:

- oversized number
- card stack
- alarm picker
- transaction sheet
- bottom action control
- tactile timeline

## Interaction Stance

- tactile pressed controls
- stateful sheets
- kinetic numbers
- drag or snap motion
- clear selected states

## Good Components

- oversized number screen
- card stack wallet
- alarm time picker
- transaction dashboard
- bottom action system
- tactile control surface

## Avoid

- web-style top navigation
- tiny charts
- weak primary action
- generic list rows
- controls without pressed or disabled states
- stacked desktop landing sections
