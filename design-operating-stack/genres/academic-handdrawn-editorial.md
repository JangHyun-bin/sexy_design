# Academic Hand-Drawn Editorial

Source signal: Reference D.

## Use When

Use for learning tools, research products, AI writing, knowledge work, student
software, note systems, and products that should feel intelligent but human.

## Surface

- off-white paper
- blue notebook field
- lined paper
- hand-drawn black illustration
- small color fill, often pink or blue
- generous academic whitespace

## Typography

- display: bookish serif
- body: serif or readable sans
- labels: quiet sans or small serif
- proof: university or institution marks when real

## Dominant Gesture

One of:

- hand-drawn hero illustration
- notebook surface
- large serif academic statement
- paper interface metaphor

## Interaction Stance

- page-like
- gentle reveal
- tabs or notebook sections
- avoid slick finance-style motion

## Good Components

- hand-drawn centerpiece
- lined paper panel
- academic logo wall
- tabbed notebook surface
- serif section statement
- small marginal doodles

## Avoid

- fake university logos
- childish cartooning
- generic AI sparkle icons
- glassmorphism
- overpolished futuristic gradients
