# Cinematic Product Fold

## Use When

Use when the product should feel like an environment, mood, or scene before it is
explained.

## Anatomy

- atmospheric surface
- product mockup or screen as scene object
- short centered or top-aligned statement
- soft CTA
- dark continuation section

## Layout Rules

- The product should feel embedded in the scene, not pasted on top.
- Keep copy sparse.
- Use foreground/background depth intentionally.
- Avoid many equal cards in the first fold.

## Typography Rules

- Soft geometric sans or quiet grotesque.
- Small labels can be pill-like but should not look like tags from a template.
- Avoid heavy editorial serif unless the scene supports it.

## Surface Rules

- Grain, blur, horizon, low-light gradient, or photographic depth can work.
- Do not use random aurora gradients without a product relationship.

## Responsive Behavior

- On mobile, keep the scene legible and crop with intent.
- Product mockup can become taller and more central.
- Reduce parallax and heavy motion on small screens.

## Anti-Slop Checks

- Fails if atmosphere hides a weak product.
- Fails if glow is the only design idea.
- Fails if the first fold reads like a stock hero.
